Rail Lines of Tokyo and Beyond

If data disappears, you may need to reload or wait a bit. Apologies for the inconvenience.

Tokyo’s railway network is the most complex in the world, with dozens of lines operated by JR East, Tokyo Metro, Toei, and private companies. Among the most important are the Yamanote Line, which loops around the city’s core; the Chūō Line, a vital east-west axis for commuters; and the Tōkaidō Shinkansen, connecting Tokyo with Osaka and beyond. These lines represent not only transportation infrastructure, but cultural and economic arteries that shape the daily life of Japan.
